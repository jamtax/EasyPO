Important conversion notes

Text-based PDFs: Best results come from PDFs that already contain embedded selectable text.

Scanned PDFs: Image-only or scanned documents usually need OCR to produce strong DOCX, XLSX, CSV, JSON, or XML outputs. This page does not perform OCR.

Spreadsheet outputs: XLSX and CSV exports preserve extracted lines and page structure, but they do not automatically rebuild complex table geometry from every PDF layout.

Privacy model: This page is designed for browser-based processing. No authentication or remote storage workflow is included in this static version.

Recommended next step

If you want enterprise-grade fidelity, the next phase should add OCR, table detection, and server-side conversion services for higher-quality DOCX, XLSX, and XML outputs.
